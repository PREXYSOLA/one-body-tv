Our Founder

Built From Worship.
Sent To The World.

Prexy Sam Ola left his parents at age 9. He hauled water, sold bread on the streets of Kano, and opened three bakeries — all before he heard the call to ministry.

Today he is a gospel singer, songwriter, founder of the Retum Brodaz group (232K YouTube subscribers), and a tech entrepreneur who has built over 10 platforms from scratch — while working night shifts as a nurse in Ohio.

"Every song I write and every platform I build comes from the same place — the presence of God. One Body Church was not my idea. It was His."

— Prexy Sam Ola, Founder

His group's catalog exceeds 1,000 gospel songs. His first single 'Gudu Gudu' crossed one million views. And the same divine inspiration that fills his music is what built One Body Church.
